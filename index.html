Frequently Asked!

Do neural networks really work like the human brain?

Not exactly. While inspired by the brain, artificial neural networks are highly simplified models. They share some basic principles - like neurons that activate based on input from other neurons - but the details of how biological neurons work and learn is far more complex and still not fully understood.
